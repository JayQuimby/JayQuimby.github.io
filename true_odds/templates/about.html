How it works:

This Neural network is trained off of the last 10 years of historical statistics data. It is currently only predicting the outcomed of NCAAF games but more to come soon. This is a straightforward ANN that has 7 layers and around 15M params. The training and validation accuracy for the model is changing because I keep training the model on new data. The model in general trains to 75% evaluation accuracy and is doing binary classification. The numbers that are displayed on the site as "probabilites" are the softmax outputs from the model. The data is all web scraped and updated weekly.
